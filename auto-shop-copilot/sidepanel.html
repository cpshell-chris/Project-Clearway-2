How Auto Shop Copilot Works

Select any tile to open that tool. The tool fills the entire sidebar for a clean, focused experience. Tap the return button at the bottom to come back here.

TekMetric Integration
When you open a Repair Order in TekMetric, the Copilot detects it automatically — customer, vehicle, and mileage context appear in the green banner and are available to all tools.

Knowledge Assistant
Instant automotive expertise — search any part or service to get technical definitions, customer-friendly explanations, urgency levels, and advisor coaching tips.

Appointment Copilot
Schedule the next appointment in three steps — choose a date and time, select services from declined items and Preventive Maintenance Recommendations, then confirm and open directly in TekMetric.

RO Copilot
Your guide through the write-up and sales process. Four phases — Write-Up, Findings, Estimate Build, and Present & Sell — keep you on track with soft checklists and AI assists at every critical moment. Phase 5 handles anything unexpected after the sale is made.

Vehicle Consultant
Generate Customer Notes, Service Advisor Reports, or Exit Schedule Reports from TekMetric data using the Preventive Maintenance and Repairs Recommendation engine.

Ask Me Anything
Craft the perfect response to any customer question or objection — in-person or by phone — in Cardinal Plaza Shell's voice.

Culture Coach
Begin your day with a randomly selected Golden Lesson from the CPS lesson bank. Reflect, then ask the culture coach for guidance on team decisions, coaching points, or any cultural question.

Review Response
Craft professional responses to Google reviews — positive or negative — in Cardinal Plaza Shell's voice.

Service Offering Developer
Build new service offerings with AI guidance, or generate complete marketing and training materials for existing services.

Culture Coach

How Culture Coach Works

Start with the lesson. Each time you open this tool, a Golden Lesson is randomly drawn from a bank of 60 — written to bring your mind into the right place before your first customer of the day. Read it, sit with it, let it travel with you through your shift.

New Lesson draws a different lesson if today's doesn't resonate — it will never repeat back-to-back.

Ask a culture question. Type any question about a workplace decision, a coaching challenge, or a choice between two approaches. Three sample questions are generated fresh each time, inspired by the lesson of the day. Tap one to use it or write your own.

The answer opens on its own screen — clean and easy to read. Use Copy Q & A to copy both the question and answer together, or New Question to return and ask something else without changing the lesson.

Previous Answers appears above the sample questions after you've received a response — tap it anytime to return to your last answer without losing it.
